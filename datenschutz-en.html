2. General Information

2.1 Processing of Personal Data and Other Terms

Data protection applies to the processing of personal data. Personal data refers to any data that can identify you personally. This includes, for example, the IP address of your device. Processing means any operation performed with such data – e.g., collecting, storing, using or deleting. For other terms, we follow the definitions in Article 4 GDPR.

2.2 Applicable Laws – GDPR, BDSG, and TDDDG

The scope of data protection is governed by laws: the GDPR as the European regulation, the BDSG as the German national law, and the Telecommunications and Telemedia Data Protection Act (TDDDG), which has replaced the former Telemedia Act (TMG) since December 1, 2021.

2.3 How Data is Processed on this Website

Some data (e.g., IP address) is automatically collected when you access the website. Other data you provide actively (e.g., contact form). Additionally, certain data is collected through interactions (e.g., cookies).

2.4 Your Rights

You have several rights, including the right to information, deletion of your data, and the right not to be contacted further.

2.5 Data Protection – Our Approach

For us, data protection is more than just a legal obligation! Your data belongs to you, and we handle it carefully. We only share your data with third parties if you have given consent or there is a legal basis.

2.6 Data Sharing and Deletion

Data is only shared if permitted by GDPR and if absolutely necessary. We delete data when the purpose for processing ceases and no further legal retention obligations exist.

2.7 Hosting

This website is hosted externally. Personal data such as IP addresses, contact requests, and access data may be stored on the host's servers. Hosting is used for fulfilling contracts and providing a secure, efficient website (GDPR Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b and f).

2.8 Legal Bases

Processing of personal data requires a legal basis:

  • Article 6(1)(a) GDPR – Consent
  • Article 6(1)(b) GDPR – Contract performance or pre-contractual measures
  • Article 6(1)(c) GDPR – Legal obligation
  • Article 6(1)(d) GDPR – Vital interests

3. What Happens on Our Website

The following data is automatically collected when accessing the website:

3.2 Cookies

3.2.1 General

We use cookies on our website. Cookies are small files stored on your device by your browser.

3.2.2 Rejecting Cookies

You can block or delete cookies via your browser settings.

3.2.3 Technically Necessary Cookies

We use technically necessary cookies to operate the website. Legal basis: GDPR Article 6(1)(f).

3.3.2 Contact

a) Email: When contacting via email, your email address and content are processed. Legal basis: consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)) or legitimate interest (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)).

b) Phone: Call data may be stored pseudonymously. Legal basis: legitimate interest (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)).

4. Other Important Information

4.1 Your Rights in Detail

  • Right to access: GDPR Article 15
  • Right to rectification: GDPR Article 16

  • Right to data portability: GDPR Article 20
  • Right to object: GDPR Article 21

Further information on your rights can also be found in the GDPR or from your data protection supervisory authority.
